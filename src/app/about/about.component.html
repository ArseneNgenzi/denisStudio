Biography

Denis Mpabuka is African contemporary artist from Rwanda,works Inema Arts Center,he is a self-taught visual artist He started painting out of a need to be financially self reliant, as well as wanting to have a lifestyle that allowed him to express himself through his work. His inspiration stems from the various cultural identities he encounters through his own heritage, traveling experiences, and his surroundings. His work can be found at the Inema Art Gallery in Kigali, Kacyiru.

Denis Mpabuka is gifted in creating narrative-centred paintings. His work marries the real and the abstract, which results in visuals that are at once familiar and deeply engaging. With an eye for details, Denis uses colourful strokes to express a variety of moods which pertain to each unique piece.

Custom Artworks

A custom commissioned artwork is a great option if you are looking to tailor a piece to suit your space.

Over the years I have created many bespoke artworks to suit homes around the world. I am experienced in working closely with individual collectors to produce a unique piece or set that perfectly fits each space.

Required information:

Size

To begin with we need to establish the quantity and size ideal for your space.

If you are unsure you may like to email photo's of the room/ space and wall measurements for input on finding the perfect size.

Orientation

Select whether your artwork will be hung landscape or portrait.

Composition and colour palette

As a starting point please select 1-3 images of my previous artworks that you connect with and email screen shots through with your quotation request. Further discussions on composition and colour selection will follow from there.

I welcome my collectors input & each quotation allows for up to 2 minor compositional amendments during the process. Further changes can be requested at an additional fee (quoted separately).

Commission Process

A limited number of scheduled commission slots are offered per year. Mailing list subscribers will be notified when the next round of slots will be available for booking. I will confirm my earliest availability on your inquiry. Generally custom pieces take 3-5weeks to complete.

Deposit

To secure your scheduled slot, a 50% deposit is required prior to commencing work. Payable via visa and other means of payments . Balance is due on completion (prior to shipping/ pick up)

Progress

Let the magic happen! Throughout the creation process you will be kept updated with progress pictures and video direct from the studio for your reference & consideration. You literally get to watch your original coming to life!

Currently, Denis has an ongoing series of paintings titled "1000 faces" showcasing a semi-abstract portrait style of beautiful faces. His use of abstract colours and facial features blur the lines of conventional beauty within the context of cultural and ethnic standards throughout the world. Each face is inspired by the vast multitude of people Denis has encountered throughout his life. He expresses the remarkable beauty he sees in each one of them, thus making every portrait completely unique. Denis Mpabuka aims to produce 1000 individual faces to complete this series.

On a day to day basis, Denis Mpabuka dedicates his time to mentoring young aspiring artists by teaching art classes at the Inema Art Centre. Furthermore, he contributes his efforts in running day to day sales in the Inema Art Gallery.
